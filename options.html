AI Intent Inference

When you dismiss or ignore the intent bubble, WhyTab can automatically infer why you opened the tab using Groq. Enter your Groq API key to enable this feature — it's free with no credit card required.

Your key is stored locally and never leaves your browser. Get a free key at console.groq.com.

Auto-close Stale Tabs

Automatically close tabs you haven't visited after a set number of days. Pinned tabs and whichever tab is currently active are never closed.

Tabs not visited for this many days will be closed during the hourly background check.
